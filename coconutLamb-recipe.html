Grenadian Curry Goat

Made with substantial goat flesh cooked till extra soft, fresh spices, and so much flavor, this rich and satisfyingly delicious Curry Goat is best served with rice and peas.Take a bite out of the Islands !

30 mins

To begin, curry goat is a delicious and filling stew prepared with robust goat meat and curry powder. Aromatics, various spices, and potatoes are all common ingredients. Curry goat is also cooked for a few hours to ensure that the goat flesh is totally soft. It practically crumbles and absorbs all of the wonderful flavors it was cooked in. This meal is popular in the Caribbean, India, and Southeast Asia.

A decent pot of curry goat should always be served with rice and peas, according to any Islander! Aside from being a part of my culture, I enjoy how comfortable this meal is. The gravy is exceptionally thick and delicious, and it has a lot of flavor. The goat flesh is fork-tender, and the potatoes offer a layer of comfort.

Instructions

Step 1

Trim meat, cut into bite-size pieces and wash in a mixture of water and vinegar.

Step 2

Add 1 tablespoon curry powder, chopped onion, escallion, garlic, ginger, thyme, scotch bonnet pepper, black pepper and salt; rub the seasonings into the meat, cover and put to marinate for two hours.

Step 3

Heat vegetable oil in a heavy bottom skillet over medium heat and add the remaining one tablespoon curry powder. Add the marinated meat and allow to sear. Turn the meat and add 4 cups boiling water. Cover and allow to simmer for about 1 hour 20 minutes or until the meat is tender.

Step 4

Add the diced potato and cook for 5 minutes. Fold in the sliced carrot and pimento berries and cook for five minutes.

Ingredients

3 pound(s) goat meat

2 tablespoon(s) Grace White Vinegar

2 tablespoon(s) curry powder

1 medium onion, chopped

2 stalk(s) escallion, chopped

4 clove(s) garlic, chopped

1 teaspoon(s) ginger, chopped

2 teaspoon(s) thyme, chopped

1/2 whole scotch bonnet pepper, chopped

1 teaspoon(s) black pepper

1/4 cup(s) Grace Vegetable Oil

4 cup(s) boiling water

1 large Irish potato, diced

1 medium carrot, sliced

4 whole pimento berries, crushed

Tools

Bowl

Large Pot
