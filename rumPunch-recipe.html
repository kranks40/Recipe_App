Rum Punch

There are as many rum punch recipes and origin stories as there are Caribbean islands. In fact, given that most families have their own variants of this "get-the-party-started" promise, which is always served on the rocks, there could be many more. Our recipe (which is the greatest!) hails from Trinidad, and it follows all of their laws down to the last sip: Angostura bitters are required, followed by grated nutmeg as a finishing touch.

prep time

30 mins

cook time

15 mins

servings

6 servings

Tags: Pineapple Punch Biscuits Epices Au Rhum Lemon Ginger Vodka

Instructions

Step 1

In a small saucepan, dissolve water and sugar on medium heat. Remove from heat and let cool.

Step 2

In a pitcher, combine rum, juices, bitters, and syrup.

Step 3

Fill glasses half-full with ice. Pour punch. Grate a pinch of nutmeg into each glass. Enjoy!

Ingredients

1 cup raw sugar

½ cup water

1 Tbsp Angostura bitters

2-2½ cups good dark rum

½ cup lime juice

½ cup other citrus juice of choice

Tools

Saucepan
