Nutmeg Tarragon Roasted Chicken

You'll want to pinch a piece of this nicely browned roasted chicken as soon as it comes out of the oven and pop it into your mouth. I can't say I blame you. After all, the perfume of nutmeg's nutty sweetness combined with tarragon's bittersweet flavor and sweet fennel that has been filling the home will have you drooling. The chicken is soft and juicy, and everyone who eats it will find it to be a new favorite.

prep time

6 mins

cook time

1hr 30mins

servings

6 servings

Tags: Lamb Lasagna Lentils Soup Mac cheese Lasagna Penne Caprese Salad

A Weber grill book gave me the idea for the nutmeg-seasoned chicken. I recall being hesitant to use the seasoning on grilled chicken. It was a fantastic dish. I've been working hard on this recipe since I had their version of grilled chicken. Fresh tarragon, freshly grated nutmeg, and the inclusion of fennel are noticeable distinctions. These adjustments elevated the flavor profile to new heights. I usually roast it in the oven, but it can also be grilled whole or butterflied over indirect heat.

Even if you bake it in the oven, the bake time will vary. I like to cook the chicken to an internal temperature of 175 degrees Fahrenheit; the standard safe temperature for chicken is 165 degrees Fahrenheit. Also, the oven temperature will impact how long it takes to cook — I use a lower temperature of 350 degrees and was able to reach 175 degrees in just under 1 1/2 hours with my four-pounder.

Instructions

Step 1

In a small bowl, add all the marinade ingredients. Use a small spoon to mix together to form a lose paste.

Step 2

OPTIONAL: rinse and dry chicken thoroughly.

Step 3

Place the chicken on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper.

Step 4

Place the chicken on the lined sheet pan.

Step 5

Season the chicken thoroughly, including inside the cavity.

Step 6

Bake/roast the chicken until it reaches 175 degree internal temperature (or your preferred temperature – no less than 165 degrees). I baked my 4 pounder for 1 1/2 hours.

Step 7

Remove the chicken from the oven and tent loosely with aluminum foil. Rest for 10 to 15 minutes before cutting.

Step 8

Plate and serve with your favorite side dish. [See recommended sides in notes section]. Enjoy!

Ingredients

4 pounds chicken (1 whole chicken) Remove and reserve any included giblets for another use.

1 1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon fresh tarragon leaves finely chopped

5 cloves garlic crushed

3 teaspoons sea salt or kosher. Adjust for your diet.

3 teaspoons granulated sugar

2 teaspoons freshly grated nutmeg

1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

1 teaspoon fennel seeds
